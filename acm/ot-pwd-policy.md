Hey everyone,

Following last quarter's security review, we're updating how passwords work
across all JerichoInfra systems. Please read this — it affects your next login.

---

## The New Format

Your new password must be a **passphrase.** We strongly recommend using a two
word passphrase in the format of `[adjective]-[noun]`, as this provides a
balance between strength and memorability. 

Some examples: `silent-turbine`, `lucid-frog`, `amber-room`

A few rules:

- Must be **at least 10 characters** (including the hyphen)
- No uppercase, numbers, or symbols required — just two words and a dash
- Cannot contain "jericho," "power," "plant," your name, or your department
- Cannot be any of your last 10 passwords

That's it. We went with this format because it's easier to remember and
harder to crack than something like `P@ssw0rd1`.

---

## A Few Other Things

**Expiration:** Passwords expire every 180 days. Admins, yours expire at 90.

**If you think you've been compromised:** Change your password immediately
and shoot an email to `soc@internal.jerichoinfra.org`.

---

## What Not to Do

Please don't write your password on a sticky note, share it with a coworker,
or store it in a spreadsheet. If you need help keeping track of passwords,
we have Bitwarden licenses available — ask IT and we'll get you set up.
